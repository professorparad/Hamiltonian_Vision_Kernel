Architecture Flow

The page is static, but the diagram is designed like a developer portfolio piece: fast to load, interactive, and readable without the Python quantum stack.

1

Image Patches

Images are converted to grayscale and split into local windows for reconstruction.

2

MPS Features

Each patch is compressed into tensor-network features using SVD-style structure.

3

Position Field

Coordinates become Fourier positional encodings so patches keep spatial identity.

4

Quantum Kernel

HVK circuits emit X, Z, and correlation observables over 1D or 2D qubit layouts.

5

Decoder

Patch pixels are reconstructed while Hamiltonian energy regularizes the latent state.

Research Tracks

Three variants are framed for viewers who want the model story before reading code.

HVK1D

Nearest-neighbor Heisenberg-style correlations for the standard quantum path.

Symmetric HVK1D

U(1)-symmetric coupling with a clearer physics constraint over XX, YY, and ZZ terms.

HVK2D

A grid circuit that better reflects the spatial geometry of image patches.
